Inside the Course

01Setting up your Business

Module 1

Setting up your business


Sole proprietor or LLC? Do I need a tax ID number? How should I file my taxes? These are all questions I had when I started and I made a few mistakes out of ignorance, but you don’t have to! We’ll not only tell you how to find these answers based on the state you live in, we will provide actual resources to give you the peace of mind that you’re making the best decision for your situation.

02Branding Yourself

Module 2

Branding Yourself


Learn the basics of putting together a personal brand to utilize while networking and marketing your new business. Branding is important for many reasons, but knowing how to market yourself to your ideal client is key to getting fully booked. We’ll show you the basics to get your started without it feeling overwhelming.

03Marketing Yourself

Module 3

Marketing Yourself


The number one question we get is, “How do I find clients?” This module is a deep dive into how to network, market yourself, and find clients!

04Booking Clients

Module 4

Booking Clients


Landing dream clients is all about the discovery call! If you aren’t familiar with what a discovery call is, don’t worry, we will show you everything you need to know to book and host discovery calls with confidence. Let’s get you fully booked!

05New Client Onboarding

Module 5

New Client Onboarding


You booked the client, now what? A wonky onboarding process can get you off to a rocky start with a client. We will show you how to seamlessly onboard new clients and create a great client experience right out of the gate!

06Systemizing your Business

Module 6

Systemizing your Business


All great businesses have standard operating procedures. In order to scale without the overwhelm and burnout, you need solid systems in place right from the get go! We will show you some of our favorite tools, templates, and systems to keep things running smoothly for you and your clients. Client experience should always be number one priority!

07Scaling your Business

Module 7

Scaling your Business


You have done all of the leg work, now it is time to go from landing your first client to fully booked! Scaling your business is kind of like rolling a snowball downhill, it’s slow to start, then it will naturally build momentum and grow.
